M O T I V A T I O N

About Me

I am a creative and diligent Compositor with an eye for detail. In the last few years, I have worked with various visual effects companies based in Mexico City, working on films, advertisments and television series shown on both a local and national level.

With the experience gained from working on these, I have improved my problem solving and time management skills. I am extremely proficient in digital compositing having studied and practiced it since my Master's in the University of York, England.

Due to my inquisitive nature, I am always eager to learn and understand the techniques used to make a given video look as desired. I see new projects as new challenges and opportunities for self-development and to learn.

The quality of my work is very important to me and I strive to provide creative solutions for different challenges by harnessing my multidisciplinary skillset while working in a team or alone. Nothing makes me more excited than working with amazing experienced artists and creating fresh creative visuals.
